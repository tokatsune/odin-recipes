Cookies

Description

Who doesn't love cookies. You should make some!!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
  • 1/2 cup packed light-brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the over to 350 degrees F and line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Beat the almond butter, brown sugar and salt together in a large bowl using a hand held or stand mixer on low speed. Add eggs and beat until combined, then add chocolate chips, mixing on low to incorporate.
  3. Drop tablespoons of dough, 1-inch apart, onto the prepared baking sheets. Bake until the tops are puffy and the edges are just crisp, about 10 minutes. Trasnfer to a wire rack for cooling.
